Welcome to Tic-Tac-Toe!

How to Play

  • Objective: Get three of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) before your opponent.
  • Game Setup: The game is played on a 3x3 grid. Player 1 is 'X' and Player 2 is 'O'.
  • Turns: Players take turns placing their mark in an empty square.
  • Winning: The first player to get three of their marks in a row wins the game.
  • Draw: If all squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game is a draw.
